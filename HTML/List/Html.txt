1 . html has 2 types of list 
     1. Unordered list - where order does not matter like shopping application
         <ul>
                 <li>Item 1</li>
                 <li>Item 2</li>
                 <li>Item 3</li>
        </ul>
     2. Ordered list - where order does matter like step by step reciepe , top 10 TV shows etc.
        <ol>
               <li>Item 1</li>
               <li>Item 2</li>
               <li>Item 3</li>
       </ol>
